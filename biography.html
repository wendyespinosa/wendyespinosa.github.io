WENDY ESPINOSA 

Wendy Espinosa is an eigth grade student currently attending Miami Arts Studio in Miami, Florida. She's 14 years old and pursues a career in IT Management. In her free time, she often creates art or disassembles computers for the enjoyment of putting them back together. She was first introduced to computers when she was four years old thanks to her father's influence. Since then, she's worked on her computer skills immensely and often creates or fixes pieces for family members and joins competitions. You can often find her helping out at school during theatre performances as light or projection manager. Furthermore, she joins many clubs such as the National Junior Honor Society, SECME, and Student Council. She also holds traits like responsibility, independence, and diligence, Wendy works to help change the way people perceive technology so that everyone can very easily use it to their advantages in the future. She hopes to make a name for herself so that people can finally recognize her skills.
